Ongoing & Upcoming

Ideas I may build next

  • ThermoBench 2.0 - multi-component mixtures, uncertainty propagation, auto-comparison dashboards.
  • REB Expansion - add workload generators, cleaner comparison plots, and hooks for real robot logs.
  • GNN Revisitation - a second pass at VLE models that tests identity failures upfront and keeps the classical-property fallback honest.
  • Smart Carbon MRV System - combine simple forward models, sensor streams, and uncertainty estimates for monitoring and verification work.
  • EcoBench: Closed-Loop Microhabitat Control Testbed - a tabletop terrarium, algae vessel, or hydroponic chamber with sensors, camera logs, power telemetry, and simple light, air, misting, and water controls. First build: log what happened, then test a simple predictor and controller.
  • REB-H: Energy-Aware Mobile Robot Testbed - a low-cost ROS 2 differential-drive robot with onboard power telemetry and repeatable physical missions; extends REB from software traces into real-robot experiments measuring task success, latency, Wh/mission, compute/sensor draw, and carbon-intensity-aware autonomy trade-offs.
  • Scientific Surrogates With Error Bars - a focused ML study on when small learned models are honest about being wrong. I would start with thermodynamic and dynamical-system datasets, compare a few surrogate families, and score them by calibration and physics checks rather than point error alone.

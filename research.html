Independent Research

Where physics meets code

My independent work sits where physics meets code. I build open tools and experiments that expose how models behave and how we can trust them.

Projects

  • ThermoBench-Consist v1.0 (2025)

    Benchmark/diagnostic for validating ML equations of state. Four physics checks (monotonicity, stability, Clapeyron slope, acoustic speed), JSON scoring, guardrails near critical regions.

    Zenodo DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.17489426

  • Assessing the Limits of Graph Neural Networks for Vapor-Liquid Equilibrium (2025)

    Negative-results study showing why seemingly accurate GNNs fail global thermodynamic consistency tests; hybrid fallback with classical libraries.

    arXiv: 2509.10565

  • Energy Efficient Robotics Software (2020-2024): Systematic Literature Review (2025)

    79-study synthesis of post-2020 work on energy in autonomy stacks (planning, perception, middleware, hardware acceleration) across mobile and manipulator platforms; proposed taxonomy of energy-aware techniques; full replication package provided.

    arXiv: 2509.10565

  • REB-1: Robot Energy Benchmark v0.1 (2025)

    Micro-benchmark for measuring power and carbon footprint of robotics workloads; standardized 60-second traces and tidy logs.

    Zenodo DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.17204853
